# Upcoming Events

**NASPR 2026 Conference**  

Dr. Sarah Bloch-Elkouby will chair the 2026 <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/naspr-2026-conference/home">North American Chapter of the Society for Psychotherapy Research Conference</a>, hosted by the Ferkauf Graduate School of Psychology at Yeshiva University. The conference will take place at the Yeshiva University Museum in the Union Square area of Manhattan, New York.

The Program Committee welcomes submissions on all areas of psychotherapy research, practice, and training, with particular interest in methodologically strong research and work that examines how psychotherapy functions in real-world settings. The Program Committee invites submissions addressing all areas of psychotherapy research, practice, and training.

The submission deadline is **February 13, 2026**, at 11:59 PM ET. If you are interested, please view the <a href="https://sites.google.com/view/naspr-2026-conference/call-for-submissions_1">submission details</a>.
