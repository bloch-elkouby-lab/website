Bennett is a second-year graduate student in the Clinical Psychology program at Teachers College, Columbia University, concentrating in Neuropsychology. His interests include suicide prevention, brain injury neurorehabilitation, chronic pain therapies, molecular neurobiology, and skiing. Along with his work as an RA, Bennett also serves as Laboratory Manager of Columbia's Resilience Center for Veterans and Families. He received his Bachelor's degree in Neuroscience from Kenyon College in 2022.
