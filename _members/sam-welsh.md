Sam is a current graduate student in the clinical psychology program at Teachers College, Columbia University. He received his B.A. in History, and Philosophy, Politics, and Economics from the University of Maryland. His research interests include schizotypal personality disorder, suicide prevention, and the application of technology to psychotherapy. He has previously conducted work related to personality disorders, online social media behavior, and human relationships with the environment. In his free time, he enjoys reading, exercise, and finding new coffee spots.
