Nhi is a current graduate student in the Clinical Psychology program at Teachers College, Columbia University. She received her B.S. in Psychology from Loyola University of. Her research interests include youth mental health, barriers and logic into utilization of mental health resources, suicide prevention, therapeutic alliance, and impact of art and music on psychopathology. She also has a growing interest in policy work and advocating for mental health initiatives in NYC as a legislative intern. Outside of her academic work, Nhi enjoys attending concerts and movie showings as well as collecting records and Smiskis.
